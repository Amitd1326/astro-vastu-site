Astrology

Astrology is the study of planets, stars, and their influence on human life. With a detailed horoscope, we can understand strengths, weaknesses, and life patterns.

How can we use Astrology nowadays?

Astrology is widely used for birth chart analysis, marriage compatibility, career guidance, financial planning, and remedies for planetary doshas.

Issues generally fixed by Astrology

  • Marriage delays and compatibility issues
  • Career blockages and job instability
  • Financial problems
  • Health concerns due to planetary imbalance
